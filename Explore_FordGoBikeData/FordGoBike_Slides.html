Insights into FordGoBike Data¶

by Jing Lu¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I looked at

  1. Demographics of users
  2. How trip duration varies across different groups of users (Subscriber vs Customer) ?
  3. How user activity level varies across different groups of users (Subscriber vs Customer) ?
  4. What are the most taken trips across different groups of users (Subscriber vs Customer) ?

Dataset Overview¶

The data, provided by Ford GoBike, a bike-sharing system, contains information of rides in the greater San Francisco Bay area. Each trip includes user info (type, gender, birth-year), time info (start time, end time, duration), location (start/end station id/name/longitude/longitude. The analysis is based on 2018's data. 110718 data points out of 1863721 are dropped due to missing information. Columns of 'start_station_latitude', 'start_station_longitude', 'end_station_latitude', 'end_station_longitude' and 'bike_share_for_all_trip' are dropped.

Demographics of Ford GoBike's Users in the Greater San Francisco Bay Area¶

  1. About 73.5% users are male, 25% are female, and 1.5% are other.
  2. About 89% users are subscribers.
  3. About 99% user <= 65 years old.

User Activity Level over Time¶

  1. Users are most active in 8:00 - 9:00, 17:00 - 18:00, followed by 9:00AM - 10:00AM, 18:00 - 19:00, and then 16:00 - 17:00 - those are rush hours.
  2. Users are more active on weekdays than weekends.
  3. Activities increase when weather gets warmer, reach and keep at a stable level during summer, climb to the peak around Oct, and then decrease as weather turns colder.

User Activity Level over Time vs Type of User¶

  1. The Subscriber group follows the pattern on the entire dataset tightly (in all three granularities: hour of day, day of week, month of year.
  2. The Customer group kind of smoothes the overall user activity level, especially in the granularity of hour.
  3. More interestingly, the Customer group is much more active than the Subscriber group over weekends and from 11:00 to 16:00. Plus, the activity of the Customer group trends up after 10:00 and reaches the peark at 17:00.

What Trips are Most Taken ?¶

How to define a trip? By start station? By end station? Or by pair of start staion and end station?

  1. By 'pair of start_station and end_station': the top 20 trips only count for 3.55% of total trips.
  2. By 'start station': top 20 trips count for 28.05% of total trips.
  3. By 'end station: top 20 trips count for 30.27% of total trips.
  4. 'Top 20 trips by start station' and 'Top 20 trips by end station' have a large degree of overlap : 19 out of 20
